About Christopher

I don't teach theory. I teach what I've lived.

Most coaches have read the books. I've built the companies, led the teams, navigated the exits, and done the unglamorous work of building something real from the ground up.

My coaching is grounded in three decades of lived experience across business, health, relationships, and community. When we work together, you get someone who has been in the room — and can help you perform at your best in it.

I'm direct, efficient, and allergic to fluff. I believe your time is your most valuable asset. I don't waste mine, and I won't waste yours.

Personal Development
For over a decade I served as Camp Director for Sunshine Nevada, leading inclusive summer camps for children and young adults with autism. Community service isn't something I do — it's who I am. I've supported multiple Nevada-based charitable organizations and know what it means to show up for people who need it.

Professional Growth
I've co-founded and scaled multiple companies in the U.S. and internationally. In 2018, my business partner and I exited LogoMix.com after eight years of deliberate, disciplined growth. I've hired, fired, pivoted, failed, rebuilt, and succeeded. That full-spectrum experience is what I bring to every client engagement.
03
Health & Relationships
True performance starts with your body and your relationships. I've invested in both — not as a side project, but as a non-negotiable foundation. When your health, your mindset, and your closest relationships are strong, everything else becomes possible. I'll help you build that foundation.

The Framework

Five Areas. One Life. Everything Connected.

Most people treat health, work, relationships, and community as separate categories. They're not. They're a single system — and when one area is neglected, the rest eventually feel it.


My coaching framework addresses all five areas of life, because sustainable growth requires a strong foundation at every level. We don't just optimize your business. We make sure the person running it is operating at full capacity too.


This is what separates coaching that changes your numbers from coaching that changes your life.
